Information We Collect

Mindful Steps is a mindfulness and walking tracking application. We collect:

  • Walking data (steps, distance, duration)
  • Walk logs and routes
  • Photos you take during walks (stored locally)
  • Your Google profile information (name, email, profile picture)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our walking tracking service
  • Store your walking data and progress
  • Authenticate you with Google Sign-In
  • Synchronize data across your devices (optional)

Data Storage

Your walking data is stored:

  • Locally on your device by default
  • Optionally in cloud storage if you choose to sync
  • Google authentication data is handled securely through Google's OAuth service

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt out of data collection
